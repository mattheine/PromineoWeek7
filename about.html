The winter time is a cold and often dreary part of the year for many. I know that if I dont get out and move my body I fall at risk of going insane while locked inside four walls. Below is a list of reasons why it is important to go skiing:

  • The thrill
  • Great exercise
  • Fun with friends
  • Meditative- Free the mind!
  • A healthy hobby
  • Snow Bunnies
  • Powder Days
  • Apres - French for Party!
